Who We Are

A people raised up to pray

The Prophetic Prayer Army is a fellowship of believers committed to a lifestyle of prayer, prophetic intercession, and consecration. Across generations and nations, we gather to seek the face of God, war in the spirit, and watch Him move with signs and wonders.

Whether you are new to prayer or a seasoned intercessor, there is a place for you in this army. Come and grow with us as we contend for breakthrough in our homes, our communities, and the world.
